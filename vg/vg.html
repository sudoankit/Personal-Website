"Above all, video games are meant to just be one thing: Fun for everyone."- Satoru Iwata, Nintendo President.

Video games are indeed interactive snapshots of our imagination as recently I have gone through a rain of giant skulls, antibiotics for machines and gliding on Saturn’s frozen rings for lust.

Rhythm of peoples hearts. There’s so much beauty in them. From babies to old people, hearts tell a lot of lovely stories.

This page is a collection of my stories, roughly chronologically, I had with video games.

Caveat: This is a personal list and thus favors what I enjoy.

I consider gameplay (good fluid combat or exploration, unique mechanics, puzzle solving), level design, difficulty and atmospheric story telling more than anything else.

I dislike checklist, spoon-fed, RNG side-quest padded stuff, turn based strategy titles (except Pokemon and few other), most online multiplayer titles esp. battle royales, rhythm based, heavy RNG and card based games, etc.

My favorites include nearly all Zeldas and Marios (platformers), some Metroidvanias, to some extent Naugty Dog titles, some CoD Single Player campaigns and DOOM, many indie puzzlers and recent FromSoft games.
I also do enjoy few action rpgs and RTS titles. I find collectathons and looter-shooters/slashers alright.

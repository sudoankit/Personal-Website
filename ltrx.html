Literary Experiments

My collection of short stories dreaming to be awake in a hardcover held by your hands. Mostly science fiction, far-fetched ideas, sometimes melodramatic, sometimes kafkaesque, often wild.

My biggest inspirations to write remain Enid Blyton, Andy Weir, Ted Chiang, Fyodor Dostoevsky, John Steinbeck, Charles Dickens & Herman Melville.

Everything here is fiction - any resemblance to real people, events, etc is purely coincidental.

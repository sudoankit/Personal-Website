2023

(In Progress) 21st Century Brain Vomit
I write about the addiction that resembles the plague - social media, mobiles & being a "consoomer". Static Death. Cross-sectional incisions of dreams of young kids reveal dull imagination but strong worship of pop culture. Rumours that fuel violent spats on the internet, cancel culture, shallow, incendiary news blasted at 110 decibels all grow that malignant disease, commonly known as depression. Is it too late to irrigate these fertile minds?

(In Progress) Runs, Marathons, & Out Of Breath
I write about the madness around India's entrance examinations which propel coaching businesses to record profits, dilute curiosity, burden students with stress and depression but also (em)power the dreams of poor and middle class households which make up most of young Indians below 18.

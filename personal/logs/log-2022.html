    • Salt and Sanctuary, Highly Recommended, 21 hours = main story + 90% side content, (NG++ till 7 available)
      2D Souls from 2 great souls!
      • Absolutely inspired by From Soft games, Bloodborne and Souls, the hunter cape is available!
      • Pretty much 2D Dark Souls
      • Great sense of fear and reward from exploration
      • Almost 100 enemies, ~15 areas with loot and secrets
      • A bit of a grind for materials which are required to upgrade weapons
      • Quite unbalanced late game, 4-5 weapons can pretty much get you through the game
      • Many attributes, huge skill tree and more to experiment and customize, play however you want apart from ranged, ranged is useless
      • Ez quick guide
        • This is just my build, play whatever you fancy!
        • Paladin Class is just makes the game much easier, focus on strength and endurance, a bit on willpower for the item find bonus.
        • Take the extra exp gain ring in the start
        • Order of Three Greed till you reach Cran's Pass, switch to Greed for most upgrade items + goldenwine
        • Brightcoral ring + kismet ring + an a bit willpower gives you ~100+ item find rate
        • Greathammers and Greatswords pretty much kill anything
          • Kureimoa(very early in game)->Jaws of Death->Scharfrichter and Warmhammer(early in game)->Obsidian Pillar(+sacred page)->Trinity Sceptor kill everything with bloodcharm + 50 STR + goldenwine from greed clan
        • Run, play with as little armour as possible -> you're fast, generate stamina faster and can roll, jump, dash faster, huge difference for farming and sometimes even bosses/one time enemies
        • Your original gear + Chef's Set and Blacksmith's set and Umbral Armour and Split Set and maybe Sohei Set, Merchant, are enough.
          • Chef's Set = highest poison def, 2 areas are very poisonous
          • Blacksmith = highest fire def, most areas/boss in mid game are fire type, esp. the one above the castle
          • Umbral = highest arcane def, great looks and one of the best in game. Buy after defeating Crasjaw from dude at the top of the Castle. Very heavy armour. Need Heavy Armour 4
          • Split set, quite good, not very heavy
        • Parry or shield or block are really not necessary
        • Toughest boss is pretty much Witch of Lake, Siam Lake. Other hard bosses: The Third Lamb, The Tree of Men
          • Witch of Lake: goldenwine, Scharfrichter VI + some parts of umbral armour to be reasonable light
            • High arcane damage so get good armour
            • Be extremely careful of the stars attack, can be fully dodged by being behind her
            • Other black magic attacks can be dodged mostly
            • I was Level 101 when I beat her, though, lol
        • Rings and charms give huge att/def bonuses or other very useful stats, always look/search for rings and charms.
        • Best place imo to Farming salt and money are:
          • Early game: Saltless in Village of Smiles (use greatsword like Kureimoa) or a Red Cage Run (use greathammer + extra reach charm)
          • Mid-Late game: Mal's Palace Cryptkeeper, short, quick from sanctuary, donate a merchant and leader for bonus!
            • Remember Cryptkeepers are very hard if your stats aren't great! and has an extreme defence to slash! USE GREATHAMMERS!
            • Greathammer + goldenwine + sacred page at ~STR 50, takes 2-3 hits to kill it
            • Easy 100k salt in 10-15 mins.
        • Be careful for:
          • Traps, look for a thin wire in the ground
          • All Knights are dangerous
          • Clay Hybrid, extremely dangerous
          • Drowned Porcelain, almost 1 hit KO if you don't have enough health or def
          • Emberskull, Bats etc (flying) are iiritating
          • Gravewalker, very hard hitting. Can be 1 hit killed using Trinity Sceptor VI + 65 STR + charms, rings.
          • Heartseeker
          • Lepris
          • Lietch
          • Mimku
          • Poison Cytoplasm
          • Retchfeeder
          • Saltless
          • Split Swordsman
          • Whisperman
        • Don't panic if you loose salt.
        • Always spend salt, level up!
        • Don't worry about gold or resurrection cost.

      An amazing, beautiful, atmospheric and soulful journey into the unknown.
      • Complete 100%(?) in ~2.5 hours.
      • Astoundingly beautiful world, atmosphere. Excellent world building.
      • Simple puzzles, I appreciate the design choice to keep this simple as the main experience is the journey you take.

Personal.

Space for my interests, views and hobbies. Obviously, highly subjective!

This side project is an experiment, a practice to improve my writing, my observations, express my thoughts and hopefully get going with my niche interests - academic film criticism, literature and art. I am also writing a book!

Ultimately my goal is to understand what makes creative arts so humane that AI can't understand. Human creative skill and imagination is beautiful, vast, exciting yet incredibly complex to recreate artificially. Every text, image, video or sound an algorithm produces is soulless.

Will bespoke human art always triumph artificial beauty or the horizons converge?
